## Overview

As Senior Product Designer for Monetization at Atlassian, I designed growth experiments that increased Jira and Confluence Premium trial activations by 30% and contributed to a 7% revenue increase in FY21 Q2 & Q3.

## The Challenge

Improving trial activation rates and converting users to Premium subscriptions while maintaining a focus on user value.

## The Solution

Through careful experimentation design, user research, and data analysis, I developed and tested growth experiments that effectively communicated the value of Premium features.

## Impact

- Increased Jira and Confluence Premium trial activations by 30%
- Contributed to a 7% revenue increase in FY21 Q2 & Q3
- Designed and executed multiple growth experiments

## Key Learnings

Experimentation is key to understanding what resonates with users. Data-driven design decisions combined with user research lead to better outcomes.
