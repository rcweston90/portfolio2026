## Overview

As Design Lead for Hedge Fund Services at Northern Trust, I worked across nine product and engineering teams to redesign the Hedge Fund Services platform, resulting in significant improvements in performance, functionality, and usability.

## The Challenge

The existing platform needed significant improvements in performance, functionality, and usability to better serve hedge fund clients.

## The Solution

By working closely with product and engineering teams, I led a comprehensive redesign that addressed user needs while improving technical performance.

## Impact

- Worked across nine product and engineering teams
- Redesigned Hedge Fund Services platform with significant improvements in performance, functionality, and usability
- Improved user experience for hedge fund clients

## Key Learnings

Large-scale platform redesigns require careful coordination across multiple teams. Understanding user needs and technical constraints is essential for success.
