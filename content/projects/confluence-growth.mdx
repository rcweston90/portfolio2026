## Overview

As Design Lead for Confluence Growth at Atlassian, I lead a team of 15 designers and 3 managers, driving improvements across the customer journey and collaborating with executives to develop strategies leveraging personalization and Atlassian Intelligence (AI).

## The Challenge

Confluence needed to improve growth rates across the customer journey while maintaining a focus on user experience and product quality.

## The Solution

I led the design team in developing strategies that leverage personalization and AI to enhance the customer experience and drive growth. By focusing on data-driven design decisions and cross-functional collaboration, we were able to make significant improvements.

## Impact

- Drove 13% improvement in growth rates across the customer journey
- Led a team of 15 designers and 3 managers
- Collaborated with executives to develop strategies leveraging personalization and Atlassian Intelligence (AI)

## Key Learnings

Leading a large design team requires balancing strategic vision with tactical execution. The combination of personalization and AI presents new opportunities for creating more intuitive and helpful user experiences.
