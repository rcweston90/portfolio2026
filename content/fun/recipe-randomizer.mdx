## The Problem

Standing in front of the fridge, staring at random ingredients, wondering what to make for dinner. Every. Single. Day.

## The Solution

A simple app where you:
1. Enter ingredients you have
2. Get recipe suggestions
3. Spin the wheel if you can't decide!

## Features

### Ingredient Matching
Smart matching that understands "chicken" includes "chicken breast", "chicken thighs", etc.

### The Wheel of Destiny
Can't decide between 5 recipes? Let the wheel choose your fate.

### Dietary Filters
Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free options.

### Save Favorites
Found a winner? Save it for later.

## Tech Stack

- React with Vite
- Spoonacular API for recipes
- Framer Motion for the wheel animation
- Local storage for favorites

## Fun Stats

- 500+ unique visitors
- Most popular ingredient combo: chicken + rice + random spice
- The wheel has made 2,000+ decisions for indecisive cooks
